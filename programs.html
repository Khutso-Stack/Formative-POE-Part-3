Health and Nutrition

Feeding schemes provide daily meals, vitamin distributions, and health awareness campaigns for children and families — addressing malnutrition and improving access to health services.

Child Protection & Well-being

Programs tackle gender-based violence prevention, support for orphaned and vulnerable children, and provide counseling services to foster resilience and life skills.

Education & Development

We help improve school attendance, supply uniforms, and create homework centers for children in need to support long-term academic growth.

Community & Family Strengthening

Supporting families through economic empowerment, home-based gardens, and community-based savings and loan groups to build resilience and independence.

How We Measure Impact

We use a mix of quantitative metrics (vaccinations, enrollment rates, literacy scores) and qualitative insights (testimonials, case studies, focus groups) to evaluate progress. KPIs include stable housing placements, improved nutrition markers, and school attendance. Transparency is maintained with quarterly reports.
