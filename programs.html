Search by type, focus area or keyword.

Health and Nutrition

Feeding schemes provide daily meals, vitamin distributions, and health awareness campaigns for children and families — addressing malnutrition and improving access to health services.

Child Protection & Well-being

Programmes tackle gender-based violence prevention, support for orphaned and vulnerable children, and provide counselling services to foster resilience and life skills.

Education & Development

We help improve school attendance, supply uniforms, and create homework centres for children in need to support long-term academic growth.

Community & Family Strengthening

Supporting families through economic empowerment, home-based gardens, and community-based savings and loan groups to build resilience and independence.

How We Measure Impact

We use a mix of quantitative metrics and qualitative insights to evaluate progress, including stable housing placements, improved nutrition markers, and school attendance. Transparency is maintained with quarterly reports shared with partners and donors.
