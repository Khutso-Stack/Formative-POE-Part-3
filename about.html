Who We Are

Helping Hands Children’s Shelter is a community-based organisation that supports vulnerable children through safe shelter, nutrition and educational support programmes.

Our Mission

To create a safe, nurturing environment where every child can grow, learn and dream beyond their circumstances.

Our Vision

A South Africa where no child is left without care, protection or opportunity.

How We Work

We work closely with parents, caregivers and social workers to ensure children receive holistic support at home and at the shelter.

We partner with local schools, clinics and faith-based organisations to refer children and coordinate services.

We follow clear governance processes, keep accurate records and provide regular reporting to funders and stakeholders.
